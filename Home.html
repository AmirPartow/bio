About Me

Welcome to my blog! I was born on July 26, 1998. My mom chose my name, Amir, after her great-grandfather, who was also named Amir. I don't have a nickname, and my last name, Partow, comes from my father's family. I am currently years old.

Cultural Identity and Migration Experience

I am originally from Turkey, since my parents are from there, but I am Persian by heritage. I was born in Iran and raised in Shiraz, a beautiful city in Fars province. In April 2021, during the peak of COVID-19, I immigrated to Canada because all of my siblings live outside of Iran. My parents still live in Iran, but they sometimes travel here to visit us.

Balancing Work, Education, and Future Goals

Currently, I work as an Uber driver, which I have been doing for three years. I chose this job because it allows me to be self-employed, and it gives me the flexibility I need while studying English in college. I recently completed the LEAP (Learning English for Academic Purposes) course after 1 year and 4 months. I have also signed up for an Associate Degree in Computer Science, starting in September 2024 at Langara College. My plan is to eventually transfer to a university. Additionally, I hold an Associate Degree in Medical Engineering from Iran.
