Kish Island, Hormozgan, Iran

Located in the Persian Gulf, Kish Island is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture. During my visit, I explored famous sites like the Greek Ship, Toranj Marine Hotel, and enjoyed a relaxing day at Simorgh Beach Park.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is a city of wonders with its unique architecture and luxurious lifestyle. I had an amazing experience visiting the Burj Al Arab, and I stayed at the Royal Central Hotel on The Palm. The city's skyline is absolutely mesmerizing, especially at night.

Vancouver, BC, Canada

Vancouver has always fascinated me with its natural beauty and vibrant community. I visited various places such as Bowyer Island, Langara College, Harrison Hot Springs Resort, Twin Falls Bridge, and the Baden Powell Trail. Each place offered a unique experience and a closer connection to nature.
